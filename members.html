BTS consists of 7 members, RM, Jin, Suga, J-Hope, Jimin, V and Jungkook.

RM:

RM or Kim Namjoon is the leader of the group. RM released his first solo mixtape, RM in 2015. In 2018, he released his second mixtape, Mono which ranked at number 26 on the US Billboard 200 and became the highest-charting album by a Korean soloist in history. He has also collaborated with artists such as Wale, Younha, Warren G, Gaeko, Krizz Kaliko, MFBTY, Fall Out Boy, Primary and Lil Nas X.

Jin:

Kim Seok-Jin, also know as Jin is the oldest member of the band. Jin has released three solo tracks with BTS "Awake", "Epiphany", and "Moon" all of which have charted on South Korea's Gaon Digital Chart. In 2019, Jin released his first independent song, the digital track "Tonight". Apart from singing, Jin has appeared as a host on multiple South Korean music programs from 2016 to 2018. In 2018, he was awarded the fifth-class Hwagwan Order of Cultural Merit by the President of South Korea along with the other members of BTS, for his contributions to Korean culture.

Suga:

Suga or Min Yoongi or Agust D is the main rapper of the band. In 2016, he released his first solo mixtape, Agust D. The album reached number three on Billboard's World Albums Chart. In 2020, he released his second solo mixtape, D-2. The mixtape ranked at number 11 on the US Billboard 200, number seven on the UK Albums Chart and number two on Australia's ARIA Album Chart.

J-Hope:

Jung Ho-seok or J-Hope is the main dancer of BTS. J-Hope released his first solo mixtape Hope World in 2018. The album got positive recactions from critics, and ranked at number 38 on the Billboard 200 in the United States, making him the highest-charting solo Korean artist on the ranking at the time. He became the first member of BTS to enter the Billboard Hot 100 as a soloist in 2019, when his single "Chicken Noodle Soup", featuring singer Becky G, debuted at number 81 on the chart.

Jimin:

Jimin or Park-Jimin is the lead vocalist of the group. Under BTS, he has released three solo songs "Lie", "Serendipity" and "Filter". "Lie" was released in 2016, as part of the group's second Korean studio album, Wings. It was described as stunning and dramatic. "Serendipity", released on the Love Yourself: Her was soft and sensual, unraveling the joy, conviction, and curiosity of love. "Filter", from the group's 2020 studio album, Map of the Soul: 7, was very different from the others, with a distinct Latin pop-esque flair and lyrics that reflected on the different sides of himself that Jimin shows to the world and those around him.

V:

V also know as Kim-Taehyung was the secret member of BTS. In May 2018 he released his solo song, "Singularity" as the trailer for BTS' then upcoming third LP, Love Yourself: Tear. The track made its UK radio debut on BBC Radio on October 25. A month after its release, The Guardian added "Singularity" to its "Top 50 songs for the month of June 2018" playlist and Billboard included it in their Critics' list of the "Top 50 BTS songs" at number 28. Overall, "Singularity" was generally well received by critics and as a result was mentioned on several year end Critics' Choice lists. The New York Times ranked it twentieth on its list of "The 65 Best Songs of 2018" alongside "Fake Love" and LA Times Pop Music Critic Mikael Wood named it the fourth "best and most replay worthy song of 2018". Guardian Music Critic Laura Snapes included it as one of her favourite tracks in their â€œBest Music of 2018: Albums and Tracks" list.

Jungkook:

Jungkook alos know as Jeon-Jungkook or Golden Maknae is the youngest member of BTS. Under BTS, he has sung three solo songs, the first "Begin" from the 2016 album Wings. The second, a future bass song titled "Euphoria", was released with a nine-minute short film on April 5, 2018, as the introduction to the third part of BTS' "Love Yourself" series. The song charted at number five on the Billboard Bubbling Under Hot 100. Its full studio version was included on BTS' Love Yourself: Answer compilation album, released on August 24. The third solo, "My Time", off the band's 2020 studio album Map of the Soul: 7, is a strobing R&B song about forgoing teenage experiences because of his career, and charted at number 84 on the US Billboard Hot 100. "Euphoria" and "My Time" are the first and second longest-charting solo tracks among K-pop singers on the Billboard World Digital Song Sales chart, having spent a record 90 and 85 weeks respectively on the ranking. In 2022 he colabarated with Charlie Puth for the song Left and Right.
