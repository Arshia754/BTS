BTS, also known as the Bangtan Boys, is a South Korean boy band that was formed in 2010 and debuted in 2013 under Big Hit Entertainment. It is originally a hip hop group but their musical style has evolved to incorporate a wider range of genres. The members are Jin, Suga, J-Hope, RM, Jimin, V, and Jungkook who are co-writes and co-produces of their own output. Their lyrics often discuss mental health, the troubles of school-age youth and coming of age, loss, the journey towards loving oneself and individualism.
